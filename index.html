Weather and map privacy policy

Globe Stations

Globe Stations is built for aviation weather awareness, maps, station lookup, and operational reference tools. The app is designed to keep personal information out of the product: no account is required, no data is sold, and location access is only used when you choose features that need it.

Last updated: 1 July 2026

METAR

At a Glance

  • Globe Stations does not require an account or ask for your name, email address, or payment details.
  • Location access, when enabled, is used for local weather and map features and is not stored by us.
  • Anonymous diagnostics may be used only to understand crashes, performance, and app reliability.

Information We Collect

Globe Stations does not collect personally identifiable information. You can use the app without creating an account, providing contact details, or sending personal profile information to us.

Anonymous Diagnostics

We may receive limited anonymous usage, crash, error, device type, operating system, and app version information to help diagnose issues and improve performance. This data is not used to identify you.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, contact information, payment details, identity-linked device identifiers, browsing history, or behavior outside Globe Stations.

Location

If you grant location permission for weather or map features, your location is used solely to show relevant local information. It is not stored by us or transmitted to us as personal data.

How Information Is Used

Anonymous diagnostics are used only to fix bugs, understand general reliability trends, and improve the app. We do not use data for advertising, profiling, or cross-app tracking.

Billing and Payments

If Globe Stations is offered through the Apple App Store with paid features or subscriptions, Apple handles all billing and payment processing. We do not collect or store payment card details.

Data Sharing and Selling

We do not sell, rent, trade, or transfer personal data to third parties. We do not share information with advertisers or marketing companies.

Third-Party Services

Globe Stations may display aviation, map, and weather information from third-party providers. Those services are governed by their own privacy policies. Globe Stations does not pass personal information to them for marketing.

Data Retention

Because we do not collect personal data, there is nothing for us to retain or delete on a personal level. Anonymous aggregated diagnostics may be retained for a reasonable period to track app reliability.

Children's Privacy

Globe Stations is suitable for general audiences and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

Even though Globe Stations collects minimal information, we use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information processed through the app.

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated from time to time. When changes are made, the updated version will be posted here and the date at the top of this page will be changed.

Contact

For privacy questions about Globe Stations, contact Rasmus Sundsten at rasmus@sundsten.fi.
