--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII). We do not
require you to create an account, provide your name, email address, or any
other personal details to use our apps.

We may collect limited, anonymous usage and analytics data to help us
understand how the apps are used and to improve their performance. This may
include:

  - General usage patterns (e.g., which features are used most frequently)
  - Crash reports and error logs
  - Device type and operating system version
  - App version

This data is fully anonymized and cannot be used to identify you as an
individual.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

2. INFORMATION WE DO NOT COLLECT

We do not collect:

  - Your name, email address, or any contact information
  - Your precise or approximate location (unless you explicitly grant
    permission for weather purposes in Global Stations / Stations, in which
    case the data is used solely to display local weather and is not stored
    or transmitted to us)
  - Payment or billing information
  - Device identifiers tied to your identity
  - Browsing history or behavior outside of our apps

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

3. HOW WE USE INFORMATION

The anonymous analytics data we collect is used solely to:

  - Diagnose bugs and crashes
  - Understand general usage trends
  - Improve the apps' features and performance

We do not use any data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond
improving our apps.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

4. BILLING AND PAYMENT

Global Stations, Global Studio, and Global Suite are offered as monthly
subscriptions through the Apple App Store. All billing and payment processing
is handled entirely by Apple. We do not collect, process, or store your
payment information (such as credit card numbers or billing addresses) at
any point.

For information on how Apple handles payment data, please refer to Apple's
Privacy Policy at https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

5. DATA SHARING AND SELLING

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise transfer any data to third parties.
We do not share any information with advertisers or marketing companies.

We may use trusted third-party analytics tools (such as Apple's built-in
analytics framework) that collect anonymous usage data on our behalf. These
tools are contractually bound to use data only for the purposes described in
this policy.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

6. DATA RETENTION

Because we do not collect personal data, there is nothing to retain or delete
on a personal level. Anonymous, aggregated analytics data may be retained for
a reasonable period to track app performance trends and is then discarded.
