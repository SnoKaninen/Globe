PRIVACY POLICY
Globe Stations
Also known as: Stations
Last updated: 1 July 2026

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

OVERVIEW

This Privacy Policy applies to Globe Stations, also referred to as "Stations",
"the app", "we", "our", or "us".

Globe Stations is built for aviation weather awareness, maps, station lookup,
and operational reference tools. We are committed to protecting your privacy.
The short version: we do not collect personal data, we do not sell any data,
and we do not share any data with third parties for marketing purposes.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We do not collect personally identifiable information. You can use Globe
Stations without creating an account, providing your name, email address, or
sharing personal profile information with us.

We may receive limited anonymous usage and diagnostic data to help us understand
app reliability and improve performance. This may include:

  - General usage patterns, such as which features are used most frequently
  - Crash reports and error logs
  - Device type and operating system version
  - App version

This information is anonymized and is not used to identify you as an individual.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

2. INFORMATION WE DO NOT COLLECT

We do not collect:

  - Your name, email address, or contact information
  - Payment or billing information
  - Device identifiers tied to your identity
  - Browsing history or behavior outside Globe Stations
  - Your location, except when you explicitly grant permission for weather or map
    features

If you grant location permission, your location is used solely to show relevant
local weather or map information. It is not stored by us or transmitted to us as
personal data.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

3. HOW WE USE INFORMATION

Anonymous diagnostics are used only to:

  - Diagnose bugs and crashes
  - Understand general reliability and performance trends
  - Improve app features and stability

We do not use data for advertising, profiling, cross-app tracking, or any
purpose beyond improving Globe Stations.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

4. BILLING AND PAYMENTS

If Globe Stations is distributed through the Apple App Store with paid features
or subscriptions, all billing and payment processing is handled by Apple. We do
not collect, process, or store payment information, such as credit card numbers
or billing addresses.

For information about how Apple handles payment data, refer to Apple's Privacy
Policy at https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

5. DATA SHARING AND SELLING

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise transfer personal data to third
parties. We do not share information with advertisers or marketing companies.

We may use trusted analytics or diagnostics tools, such as Apple's built-in
diagnostics, that provide anonymous reliability information. These tools are used
only for the purposes described in this policy.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

6. DATA RETENTION

Because we do not collect personal data, there is nothing for us to retain or
delete on a personal level. Anonymous aggregated diagnostics may be retained for
a reasonable period to track app reliability and performance trends.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Globe Stations is suitable for general audiences and is not directed to children
under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

8. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Globe Stations may display aviation, map, and weather information from
third-party providers. Those services are governed by their own privacy policies.
Globe Stations does not pass personal information to them for marketing.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

9. SECURITY

Even though Globe Stations collects minimal information, we use reasonable
technical and organizational measures to protect information processed through
the app.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

10. POLICY UPDATES

This policy may be updated from time to time. When changes are made, the updated
version will be posted here and the date at the top of this page will be
changed.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
