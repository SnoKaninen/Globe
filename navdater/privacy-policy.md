# Privacy Policy for NAVDater

Last updated: April 4, 2026

NAVDater ("the App") respects your privacy.

## Data Collection
NAVDater does not collect, sell, or share personal data.

## Local Processing
All file operations are performed locally on your device, including ZIP extraction, file preparation, and copy operations.

## Permissions
NAVDater may request access to files, folders, or external drives only to perform actions you explicitly request.

## Analytics and Tracking
NAVDater does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or cross-app tracking.

## Data Retention
NAVDater does not maintain a cloud backend and does not retain your files outside local temporary processing required for app functionality.

## Third-Party Services
If any third-party system components are used by the operating system (for example, Apple file pickers), their data handling is governed by Apple policies.

## Children's Privacy
NAVDater is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Your Control
You can control app file access through system permission settings and revoke access at any time.

## Changes to This Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The "Last updated" date will reflect the latest revision.
